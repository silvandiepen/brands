## Sources

Every public color and asset records its source. Preferred evidence is an official guideline, official download, or official website. Community references are clearly marked and do not produce verified status by themselves.

## Modifications

Allowed technical processing is limited to deterministic cleanup, safety removal, metadata cleanup, and serialization changes that preserve visual geometry.

Open Brands does not automatically redraw, simplify, recolor full-color assets, remove trademark symbols, or create unofficial lockups.

## Corrections and takedowns

Brand owners can request removal, replacement, source correction, historical classification, or usage notes through a private process. Credible urgent disputes can disable public delivery while review continues.
