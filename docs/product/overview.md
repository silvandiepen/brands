# Product overview

Open Brands resolves a brand name, domain, alias, or stable ID to reviewed visual identity resources.

## Public surfaces

- website: `open-brands.org`;
- API: `api.open-brands.org`;
- assets: `cdn.open-brands.org`;
- npm package: `open-brands`.

## Core capabilities

- search and resolve brands;
- browse current and historical identity assets;
- inspect palettes with semantic roles and source information;
- download SVG, PNG, WebP, metadata, and design tokens;
- build selected-brand packs;
- consume the same data through the package, API, or CDN;
- propose additions and corrections through a guided pull-request workflow.

## Quality rules

- every public asset and color has a source;
- current and historical material are clearly separated;
- disputed material is not selected by default;
- the platform never implies endorsement or grants trademark permission;
- automated checks detect problems but do not invent brand facts;
- a smaller reviewed dataset is preferable to a larger opaque one.
